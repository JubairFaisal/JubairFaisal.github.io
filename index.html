Research Works

  • ED2C3-EDNet-Pneumonia Detection Framework: Developed an ultra-lightweight and high-performing framework for pneumonia detection by integrating convo- lutional neural networks (CNNs), ensemble learning, and knowledge distillation to achieve efficient and accurate diagnosis. Multiple pre-trained models were analyzed and combined based on experimental performance to enhance generalization. Datasets from two different sources were merged and extensively preprocessed to im- prove data quality, feature extraction, and model robustness. The ADASYN method was applied to balance the dataset, ensuring better learning and improved accuracy while maintaining computational efficiency. Finally, knowledge from the complex ensemble model was distilled into a simpler model, significantly reducing size and complexity while maintaining high accuracy and faster inference speed.
  • Down Syndrome Detection: Worked on Down syndrome detection in children using CNNs, transfer learning, and nature-inspired optimizers. Since early detection of Down syndrome is crucial for effective treatment, an image-based detection system was developed using facial images of children. Nature-inspired optimizers performed well for optimization, and CNNs were an excellent fit for the study. An accuracy of 99% was achieved.
