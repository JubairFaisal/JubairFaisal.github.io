Research Experience

Current Research Project    Feb. 2025 – Present

Jahangirnagar University | Yangzhou University — Dhaka, Bangladesh | Yangzhou, China

Developed an ultra-lightweight and high-performing framework for pneumonia detection by integrating convo- lutional neural networks (CNNs), ensemble learning, and knowledge distillation to achieve efficient and accurate diagnosis. Multiple pre-trained models were analyzed and combined based on experimental performance to enhance generalization. Datasets from two different sources were merged and extensively preprocessed to im- prove data quality, feature extraction, and model robustness. The ADASYN method was applied to balance the dataset, ensuring better learning and improved accuracy while maintaining computational efficiency. Finally, knowledge from the complex ensemble model was distilled into a simpler model, significantly reducing size and complexity while maintaining high accuracy and faster inference speed.

Master’s Research Project    Feb. 2024 – Oct. 2024

Jahangirnagar University — Dhaka, Bangladesh

Worked on Down syndrome detection in children using CNNs, transfer learning, and nature-inspired optimizers. Since early detection of Down syndrome is crucial for effective treatment, an image-based detection system was developed using facial images of children. Nature-inspired optimizers performed well for optimization, and CNNs were an excellent fit for the study. An accuracy of 99% was achieved.

BSc Projects

Date Fruit Classification | 2023

This two-member project, submitted for CSE-480: Project Work, developed “An Image-based Android Appli- cation for Date Fruit Classification using Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)”. Using Python, we applied machine learning to classify nine types of date fruits with CNN models trained on 1,658 images. The ReLU- based model achieved 99.47% accuracy, then converted to TensorFlow Lite and integrated into an Android app with a Java interface. The app enables real-time fruit recognition from camera or gallery images, providing a fast, accurate, and automated solution for date fruit classification.
